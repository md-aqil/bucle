MVP

Minimum viable product

An MVP is a development technique where a new product is introduced in the market with basic features, but enough to get the attention of the consumers. The final product is released only after getting sufficient feedback from the product's initial users.

Core features for early adopters

Validates market demand

Basis for iterations

Feedback-driven development

6-7 Weeks Delivery Time

POC

Proof of Concept

A Proof of Concept is an exercise to test a certain hypothesis or theory that a particular idea can be turned into a reality. It's the first tangible form of your idea, often used to demonstrate functionality and to verify a certain concept or theory that can be achieved in development.

Demonstrates feasibility

Tests idea's practicality

Minimal functionality

Basis for funding opportunities

1-2 Weeks Delivery Time

Production App

A production app refers to the final, fully developed version of your app that's ready to be used by end-users. It encompasses all the features and robustness required for daily operations and is fully supported for any issues end-users may encounter.

Full feature set

Market-ready

Ongoing support and maintenance

Scalability and performance-focused

8-10 Weeks Delivery Time
